User Journey

This prototype now follows the user journey from concern to action.

01

Trigger Scene

The journey begins with a simple concern: can these medications be taken together?

02

Open App

The login screen builds trust and signals that the platform is clinical, reliable, and safe.

03

Input Items

Users enter medications, supplements, foods, and drinks while seeking confidence in data accuracy.

04

System Processing

The analysis step emphasizes intelligent recognition, database matching, and interaction review.

05

View Result

The result screen pairs a risk level with action guidance so medical terms feel easier to interpret.

06

Decision & Escalation

When risk is elevated, physician consultation helps users move from awareness to next-step action.

07

History

Profile and history views support repeat checks, reference behavior, and longer-term retention.

Pain Points

Online answers are inconsistent and hard to trust.
There is too much information and drug names are easy to mistype.
Medical terminology is hard to understand.
Being warned is not enough if the next action is still unclear.
High-risk boundaries can feel ambiguous.

Opportunities

Turn vague uncertainty into a clearer judgment.
Simplify entry with focused search and smarter recognition.
Pair risk levels with concrete action recommendations.
Offer guidance and escalation without pretending to diagnose.
Use history to support repeated queries and retention.
